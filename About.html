    • A History of Excellence


    Launching in the summer of 2003 in a small workshop with a team of 4, House of Zunn has evolved continuously and come very far from its humble beginnings. Today the company is a brand synonymous with luxury, class and quality. From creating
    large scale turn key solutions for corporate clients to providing the perfect arm chair for a living room, the company has worked on projects of all sizes.


    At Zunn we manufacture all of our furniture in house, this allows us a greater degree of flexibility when designing interiors for our clients. Our pieces are unique but timeless, we are committed to using the best materials available and ensuring a high standard for craftsmanship.


    We understand how challenging designing the perfect work or living space can be and at Zunn we aim to always deliver something beyond the expectations of our patrons.

    Our Designers

    Uzma Mirza


    An artist at heart, Uzma Mirza has been a prominent member of the design scene in South East Asia for the last two decades. Her work has spanned multiple industries and incorporated a variety of aesthetics. She is not only a powerful creative force but an engaging collaborator, continuously striving to deliver exceptional design.
